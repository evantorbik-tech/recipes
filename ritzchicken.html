ritz chicken

Ingredients

  • Sour cream
  • Ritz crackers
  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts

steps to make rites chicken

  1. crush the crackersand pour them into a shallow dish
  2. Then spray a baking sheet with cooking spray and set it aside before preheating the oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Pat chicken dry with a paper towel.
  4. Place sour cream in a shallow dish or plate and roll in the sour cream mixture. Youâ€™ll be surprised at how well it coats!
  5. Roll each chicken breast in cracker crumbs.
  6. Place on the prepared baking sheet and put in the oven.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes, flip, and then bake for 20 to 30 minutes more until golden brown.
